Network Topology at Home

Last updated on December 30, 2024 pm

Simple

flowchart TD
    0[Optical Fiber] --> 1
    1[/Optical Modem\] --> 2{{Router}}
    2 --> PC
    2 --> SmartTV
    2 --> Mobile

Normal

graph TD
    0[Optical Fiber] --> A
    A[/Optical Modem\] --> B{{Router}}
    B --> C[Switch]
    C --> SmartTV
    C --> n[(NAS)]
    B --> Mobile
    B --> D{{Access Point 1}}
    D --> Mobile
    D --> p[PC in Bedroom]
    B --> E{{Access Point 2}}
    E --> Mobile
    E --> q2[PC in Living Room]
